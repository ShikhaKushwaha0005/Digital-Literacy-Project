Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist


Do’s
1. Maintain Professional Tone

Always use respectful and polite language in your posts and comments. This helps create a positive digital image.

2. Keep Your Profile Clean

Ensure your profile picture, bio, and posts are appropriate for academic and professional environments.

3. Verify Information Before Sharing

Check the authenticity of any news or content before posting to avoid spreading misinformation.

4. Use Strong Passwords

Create strong and unique passwords for your accounts to protect them from unauthorized access.

5. Think Before You Post

Consider how your content may impact your reputation before sharing anything online.

 
 
 Don’ts
1. Avoid Sharing Personal Information

Do not share sensitive details like your phone number, address, passwords, or OTPs publicly.

2. Do Not Post Inappropriate Content

Avoid uploading content that is offensive, abusive, or unprofessional.

3. Stay Away from Online Arguments

Do not engage in unnecessary debates or conflicts that can harm your image.

4. Avoid Suspicious Links

Do not click on unknown or suspicious links, as they may lead to scams or malware.

5. Do Not Overshare
Avoid sharing too much personal information about your daily life or private matters.

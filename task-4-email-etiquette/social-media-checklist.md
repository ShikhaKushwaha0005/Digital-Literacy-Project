Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
 Do’s
Maintain a Professional Tone
Always use respectful and polite language in your posts and comments to create a positive digital image.
Keep Your Profile Clean and Appropriate
Ensure your profile picture, bio, and posts are suitable for academic and professional environments.
Verify Information Before Sharing
Check the authenticity of any news or content before posting to avoid spreading misinformation.
Use Strong and Secure Passwords
Create strong, unique passwords to protect your accounts from unauthorized access.
Think Before You Post
Consider how your content may affect your reputation before sharing anything online.
 Don’ts
Avoid Sharing Personal Information
Do not share sensitive details like your phone number, address, passwords, or OTPs publicly.
Do Not Post Inappropriate Content
Avoid uploading content that is offensive, abusive, or unprofessional.
Stay Away from Online Arguments
Do not engage in unnecessary debates or conflicts that can harm your image.
Avoid Suspicious Links
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links, as they may lead to scams or malware.
Do Not Overshare
Avoid sharing too much personal information about your daily life or private matters.
